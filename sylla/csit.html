About the college

TNew Summit College (NSC), established in 1998 (2055 B.S.), is a prominent private educational institution located in Shantinagar Gate, New Baneshwor, Kathmandu, Nepal. The college specializes in Management, Science, and Information Technology streams, offering a range of programs affiliated with Tribhuvan University and the National Examinations Board (NEB). The college boasts modern infrastructure, including three well-equipped buildings with spacious classrooms, advanced laboratories, and a comprehensive library. Situated in a safe and accessible area of Shantinagar, New Baneshwor, the campus provides a conducive learning environment. A dedicated team of experienced faculty and supportive administrative staff are committed to nurturing students' academic and personal growth.
